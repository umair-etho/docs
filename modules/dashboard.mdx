The Dashboard is your central hub for managing your business operations with AI-powered insights. Get instant clarity on what needs your attention, track performance metrics, and never miss critical opportunities or issues.

## Key Features

### Olympus Actions

AI-powered recommendations that tell you exactly what to do next, prioritized by impact and urgency.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Critical Alerts" icon="triangle-exclamation" color="#ef4444">

  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Growth Opportunities" icon="chart-line-up" color="#10b981">

### Incomplete Actions

Track unfinished tasks that need your attention with smart prioritization.

<Accordion title="How it works">
  - **Priority ranking**: Most critical tasks appear first
  - **Time tracking**: See how long tasks have been pending
  - **Quick actions**: Complete or dismiss tasks directly from the dashboard
  - **Progress indicators**: Visual status updates for multi-step tasks
</Accordion>

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Your Actions">
    Start each day by checking your Olympus Actions. Critical alerts appear at the top - handle these first to prevent revenue loss.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Priority Tasks">
    Work through incomplete actions in order of priority. Use quick actions to mark items complete or dismiss non-relevant tasks.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Monitor Your Metrics">
    Check revenue analytics and growth metrics to understand your current performance and identify trends.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Act on Recommendations">
    Implement growth opportunities and optimization suggestions to continuously improve your results.
  </Step>
</Steps>
