## How to Complete Your Codex

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start with AI Analysis">
    Let AI analyze your website first - it will pre-fill many answers based on your existing content and messaging.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Pre-filled Answers">
    Check AI suggestions and modify them to ensure they accurately reflect your brand strategy and goals.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Missing Sections">
    Fill in any gaps where AI couldn't determine your brand positioning or where you want to refine the suggestions.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Validate with Your Team">
    Share your completed Codex with key team members to ensure everyone aligns on your brand strategy.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Pro Tips for Maximum Impact

<Tip>
**Be Specific**: The more detailed your answers, the better AI can understand and replicate your brand voice in generated content.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Think Like Your Customer**: Answer questions from your customer's perspective to ensure your brand messaging resonates with your target audience.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Review Regularly**: Update your Codex quarterly to reflect business growth, market changes, and evolving customer needs.
</Tip>

<Warning>
**Don't Rush**: Take time to think through each question carefully. Your Codex becomes the foundation for all AI-generated content, so accuracy is crucial.
</Warning>
