Media is your brand's asset library - the organized collection of photos, videos, and content that brings your brand to life. Keep everything organized and accessible so you can create consistent, professional marketing materials quickly and efficiently.

## Why Organized Assets Matter

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Speed Up Campaign Creation" icon="rocket">
    Find the right assets instantly when creating campaigns, eliminating time spent searching through disorganized folders and files.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Ensure Brand Consistency" icon="shield-check">
    Organized assets help maintain visual consistency across all marketing materials, reinforcing your brand identity with every piece of content.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Scale Content Production" icon="users">
    Team members can easily find and use approved brand assets, enabling faster content creation while maintaining quality standards.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Pro Tips for Effective Asset Management

<Tip>
**Start with High-Quality Assets**: Upload your best photos and videos first to establish a strong foundation for your brand library.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Be Consistent with Naming**: Use a consistent naming convention for all assets to make them easily searchable and organized.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Regular Cleanup**: Schedule monthly reviews to remove outdated content and ensure your library stays current and relevant.
</Tip>

<Warning>
**Quality Over Quantity**: It's better to have fewer high-quality assets than many mediocre ones that could hurt your brand perception.
</Warning>
