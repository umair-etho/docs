## Pro Tips for Effective Design Systems

<Tip>
**Start with Your Brand Colors**: Choose colors that reflect your brand personality and appeal to your target audience, then build your entire system around them.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Test on Real Content**: Use live preview with actual content examples to ensure your design system works in practice, not just in theory.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Consider Accessibility**: Ensure sufficient color contrast and readable fonts to make your brand accessible to all customers.
</Tip>

<Warning>
**Don't Overcomplicate**: Start with a simple, effective design system. You can always add complexity as your brand evolves and grows.
</Warning>
