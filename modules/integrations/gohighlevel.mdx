Automatically sync leads from your funnels to your CRM for seamless customer management.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Automatic Lead Capture" icon="download">
    Every form submission from your funnels automatically creates a new lead in your GoHighLevel CRM.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Real-Time Data Sync" icon="sync">
    Customer information updates instantly across all platforms so your team always has current data.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Lead Scoring" icon="star">
    Automatically score leads based on their behavior and responses to prioritize follow-up efforts.
